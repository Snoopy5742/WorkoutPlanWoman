Workout:

Day 1: Lower Body

  • Squats: 3 sets of 10-12 reps.
  • Walking Lunges: 3 sets of 10 reps per leg.
  • Glute Bridges: 3 sets of 10-12 reps.
  • Plank: 3 sets, hold for 20-30 seconds.

Day 2: Upper Body & Core

  • Push-ups: 3 sets of 8-10 reps (can do on knees if needed).
  • Mountain Climbers: 3 sets of 10-12 reps.
  • Bodyweight Dips: 3 sets of 8-10 reps (using a chair or bench).
  • Supermans: 3 sets, hold for 20 seconds.

Day 3: Full Body

  • Squats: 3 sets of 10-12 reps.
  • Jump Lunges: 3 sets of 10 reps per leg.
  • Burpees: 3 sets of 5-8 reps.
  • Jumping Jacks: 3 sets of 30 reps.

Day 4: Lower Body

  • Squats: 3 sets of 10-12 reps.
  • Walking Lunges: 3 sets of 10 reps per leg.
  • Glute Bridges: 3 sets of 10-12 reps.
  • Plank: 3 sets, hold for 20-30 seconds.

Day 5: Upper Body & Core

  • Push-ups: 3 sets of 8-10 reps (can do on knees if needed).
  • Mountain Climbers: 3 sets of 10-12 reps.
  • Bodyweight Dips: 3 sets of 8-10 reps (using a chair or bench).
  • Supermans: 3 sets, hold for 20 seconds.

Day 6: Full Body

  • Squats: 3 sets of 10-12 reps.
  • Jump Lunges: 3 sets of 10 reps per leg.
  • Burpees: 3 sets of 5-8 reps.
  • Jumping Jacks: 3 sets of 30 reps.

Day 7: Rest
